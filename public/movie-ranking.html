The Best Movies According to Vijay

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Batman Triology

The Batman trilogy, directed by Christopher Nolan, is a masterclass in superhero storytelling.
From Bruce Wayne's journey to becoming Gotham's Dark Knight to the rise and fall of iconic villains like the Joker and Bane,
each film is a gripping exploration of justice, sacrifice, and the human psyche.

Interstellar

"Interstellar," directed by Christopher Nolan, is a breathtaking odyssey through space and time. With stunning visuals and a mind-bending plot,
the film follows humanity's struggle for survival as a team of astronauts embarks on a daring mission to find a new habitable planet. It's a cinematic journey like no other.

12 Angry Men

"12 Angry Men" is a riveting courtroom drama that unfolds almost entirely within a jury room. Directed by Sidney Lumet, the film explores themes
of justice, prejudice, and the power of persuasion as 12 jurors deliberate the fate of a young man accused of murder. It's a tense and gripping classic.
